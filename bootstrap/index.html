Color wheel

A color wheel is a circular chart that visually organizes colors based on their relationships. It shows primary, secondary, and tertiary colors arranged in a cycle, helping you understand harmony, contrast, and how different colors blend or complement each other. It’s commonly used in design, art, and UI work to choose balanced color combinations.
